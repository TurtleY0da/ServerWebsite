SMP Server Rules


Classifications

  • Tier 1 Offence: You will be instantly banned.
  • Tier 2 Offence: You will recieve one(1) warning. Should you commit another offence, you will be banned

Tier 1 Offences

  • Hacking
  • XRay
  • Theft
  • Griefing:
    Griefing includes mass TNT explosions, lava casts, covering stuff in lava or water, and any form of significant destruction of a base.
    Exception: The builder provides permission.

Tier 2 Offences

  • Theft
  • Illegal Killing:
    Illegal killing includes repeated killing, spawn killing, unsolicited killing, revenge killing, and killing that results in the destruction of the person's items (by lava, void, etc.) unless they gave you permission or you help recover lost items.

    This does not include prank kills (administrators get to determine whether a prank kill was illegal or not), war, if both parties agree to fight, or a trap in your own base/territory.

Admins decide if a rule was broken. They can ban and give warnings as they see fit, even if a rule listed here was not explicitly broken. However, Admin abuse is also not tolerated, and will result in the banning of the Admin.
